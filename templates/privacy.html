Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the Service, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued identification, or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Provide, maintain, and personalise your experience with the Service.
  • Respond to support requests or account-related queries.
  • Understand how the Service is used so we can improve it over time.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a limited number of trusted third-party services to help operate the platform, such as hosting providers. These services are only granted access to data necessary for their specific function and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
